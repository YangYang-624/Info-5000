# Findings · Introduction

## Result Highlights

- `Bounded trust-region robust search around the UA3 incumbent`: The mechanism upgrade was technically successful: the trust-region BO controller ran end-to-end, wrote full ranked outputs, and produced adaptive candidates under the unchanged evaluation contract. The scientific result was neutral. The robust winner remained UA3_3.00C_rest10m_3.80C, while the nominal-only winner remained BO_3step_conservative. Across 70 scenario evaluations, all candidates simulated successfully but 34 evaluations failed the robust guard, showing that the main bottleneck is feasibility-aware candidate quality rather than simulator execution. The closest safe adaptive candidate, UA3_TRBO_3.00C_rest10m_3.75C, passed all guards but was still slightly worse than the incumbent on the robust score. (Q30=0.0927; plating_loss=0.3218; sei_growth=57.7113; total_lli=0.014)
- `Feasibility-aware trust-region robust search beyond the UA3 incumbent`: The feasibility-aware controller produced a real scientific gain. The new robust winner is UA3_TRBO_3.00C_rest10m_3.95C, not the old incumbent UA3_3.00C_rest10m_3.80C. On the nominal surface it improved Q30 from 0.0926797696 to 0.0933852046 while preserving plating_loss and further reducing sei_growth and total_lli. In the bounded main search, the top four adaptive candidates above the old incumbent all kept guard_pass_rate 1.0, and the new winner achieved mean_score 0.0222770421 with worst_score 0.0 and robust_utility 0.0129961619. This is the first line in the project where the mechanism upgrade itself, not just local manual enumeration, materially improves the incumbent. (Q30=0.0934; plating_loss=0.3218; sei_growth=57.6462; total_lli=0.014)
- `Frontier-anchored feasibility-aware trust-region search beyond the 3.95C winner`: The main frontier-followup search confirmed UA3_TRBO_3.00C_rest10m_4.15C as the new robust top candidate. Relative to the 3.95C anchor, the nominal metrics improved to Q30=0.09440408660083764, plating_loss=0.3218373881357771, sei_growth=57.57659506258706, and total_lli=0.014026363883363733. Across the full followup_v1 bundle the winner kept guard_pass_rate=1.0, and the search controller converged at best_point=[3.0,10.0,4.15] with stop_reason=trust_region_floor. (Q30=0.0944; plating_loss=0.3218; sei_growth=57.5766; total_lli=0.014)
